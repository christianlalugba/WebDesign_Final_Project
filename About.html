About Football Agency Sierra Leone

We identify, develop and promote talented footballers from Sierra Leone to the wider world.

Who We Are

Football Agency Sierra Leone works to create pathways for players from grassroots to professional opportunities. We provide scouting, training, mentorship and placement services with integrity and passion.

Our Vision

To be the leading agency in West Africa that opens doors for Sierra Leonean talent on the global stage.

Our Mission

To develop players holistically by providing professional training, career management and exposure to clubs and scouts worldwide.

Our History

Founded by former players and coaches, we have grown from a local talent initiative to an agency connecting players with regional and international opportunities.

About Us

Football Agency Sierra Leone is dedicated to developing and promoting top talent in West African football.
